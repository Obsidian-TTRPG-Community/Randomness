# 09 - Going Further

You know the essentials. Here's what else is in the box.

## The full reference

Settings → Randomness → **Open reference** — every feature, every
syntax, one searchable page. The deep version of this guide.

## Portraits

Random character faces with rolled names, in codeblocks or inline.
Needs a one-click art pack: Settings → Randomness → **Install
Fantasy Portrait Pack**. Then try a `portrait` codeblock, or the
Portraits and Builder tabs in the sidebar.

## Fantasy Hub

A showcase bundle: town generators plus ready-to-run Templater
templates. Settings → Randomness → **Install Fantasy Hub
content**.

## Scripting

Other plugins and Templater scripts can roll your tables:

```text
app.plugins.plugins["randomness"].api
```

Roll a whole NPC into a fresh note from a template — see API.md
in the repository for everything it can do.

## Twenty years of tables

`.rdm` files are the Inspiration Pad Pro format — the community
has been writing generators in it for two decades, and they load
as-is (as do `.ipt` files). Search for "inspiration pad" tables
and drop them into your Generator root.

That's the tour. Go roll something. 🎲
