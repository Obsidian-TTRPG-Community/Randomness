# 08 - Coming From Dice Roller

Used the Dice Roller plugin? Everything you wrote still works —
Randomness speaks its language.

1. Disable Dice Roller (Settings → Community plugins).
2. Make sure Randomness is enabled.
3. That's it. Your `dice:` rolls, table rolls, tag rolls, and
   saved formulas all keep working.

Try some classic Dice Roller syntax, live:

- `dice: 1d20 + 5` — plain rolls
- `dice: 4d6dl1` — modifiers
- `dice: 3d6>=5` — success counting

What you GAIN by switching: the 🔒 lock (results saved into your
notes — Dice Roller's saved results lived in plugin data and got
lost), tag rolls without needing Dataview, and the whole
generator engine underneath.

The full switching guide (including the few things that changed)
is here:
[migrating-from-dice-roller](https://github.com/Obsidian-TTRPG-Community/Randomness/blob/main/docs/migrating-from-dice-roller.md)
